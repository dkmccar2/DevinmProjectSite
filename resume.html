Patient Advocate Health Management Mobile Application, Capstone Project

The Patient Advocate Health Management Mobile Application is designed to empower patients by connecting them with qualified patient advocates who can help navigate the complexities of the healthcare system. The app allows both patients and advocates to create detailed profiles, where advocates can showcase their expertise, pricing, and availability, making it easier for patients to find the right support for their needs. Patients can store their health information securely within the app, enabling advocates to provide tailored assistance based on individual health circumstances. With features such as secure messaging, appointment scheduling, and access to resources, the application fosters a collaborative environment where patients receive the guidance they need to make informed healthcare decisions.

  • Collaborated in agile weekly sprints & stand-up meetings to manage project progress.
  • Developed a secure login system and a robust database to store and efficiently search for patient advocate information.
  • Designed and implemented separate interfaces for both patients and patient advocates within the mobile application.
  • Developed using C# with .NET MAUI, ensuring cross-platform compatibility.

Rental Property Management Software, Class Project

Visual Basic Application for rental property management, which featured role-based access for different user types, including admin, staff, and business analysts. The system allowed for adding users with login credentials and supported the creation of property groups, which could be assigned to specific staff members. The application displayed properties by their property number, along with the current tenant balances. It also included a notification system for each property, highlighting important events such as upcoming lease renewal dates, rent due, and the accumulation of late fees for overdue payments. Additionally, the application provided functionality for generating reports on specific properties or groups of properties.

  • Developed a GUI-based Excel application with VBA for rental property management ledger.
  • Implemented role-based access control for rental management and ledger actions by staff and admins.
